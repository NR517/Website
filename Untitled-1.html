Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch is a console realeased by Nintendo in 2017. Its main appeal is that it can be used as both a portable and home console, as well as the detachable Joy-Con controllers.

Notable Games Realeased on Nintendo Switch:

  • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild
  • Super Mario Odyssey
  • Mario Kart 8 Deluxe
  • Super Smash Bros Ultimate
  • Kirby and the Forgotten Land
