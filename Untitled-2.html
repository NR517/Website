Playstation 5

The Playstation 5 is a console that was realeased is 2020 by Sony. It features high resolution graphics and a duel-sense controller that can respond to events in the game.

Notable Games Realeased on Playstation 5:

  • Spider Man 2
  • Astro Bot
  • God of War Ragnarok
  • Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart
  • Horizon Forbidden West
